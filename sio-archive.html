Stanford Internet Observatory Archive

Preserved research and analysis from the Stanford Internet Observatory (2019–2024)

The Stanford Internet Observatory studied the abuse of information technology—with a focus on social media—and produced open-source investigations, policy analysis, and peer-reviewed research. We are extremely grateful to the team at the Wayback Machine for archiving our website. Shelby Grossman used Claude Cowork to put together this website. Any errors/typos are a result of that process, and not the Wayback Machine.
